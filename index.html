Who is NF?

NF is an artist who creates music that is raw, emotional, and honest. He talks openly about struggles, fear, hope, and real life experiences.

Some of his best albums

  • Moments (2010)
  • Mansion (2015)
  • Therapy Session (2016)
  • Perception (2017)
  • The Search (2019)
  • Clouds (The Mixtape) (2021)
  • Hope (2023)
